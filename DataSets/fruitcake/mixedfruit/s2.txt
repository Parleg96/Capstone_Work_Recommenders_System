fruit cake is a delicious recipe that you can easily make at home this
variation is a perfect winter treat with a mixture of fruits and grated
carrots rich in taste it can be served during celebrations like a family get
together birthdays kitty parties and during christmas it can also be served
as an evening teatime cake the addition of mixed dried fruits and nuts makes
the cake a delectable treat in this recipe the flavours are enriched with
the addition of cinnamon and orange and strawberry essence this comforting
cake is a treat for everyone so don t waitpick up your oven mittens and
give it a try

read moreread less

ingredients of fruitcake

4 servings1 serving2 servings3 servings4 servings5 servings6 servings7
servings8 servings9 servings10 servings11 servings12 servings13 servings14

1 cup all purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 egg
1 cup mixed fruits
1 teaspoon powdered cinnamon
4 drops strawberry essence
2 tablespoon melted butter

1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/3 cup powdered sugar
1/2 cup grated carrot
4 drops orange essence
1 tablespoon vinegar
3 tablespoon water

how to make fruitcake

step 1 prepare a baking tin

to prepare this delicious recipe grease a 7 inch baking tin with melted
butter then lightly dust it with refined flour

step 2 sift the dry ingredients

after that sift the all purpose flour salt baking powder and baking soda
together in a bowl and keep it aside

step 3 beat the eggs sugar

then break the eggs into a bowl also add sugar and beat them together until
the mix is creamy

step 4 combine the flour mix with egg sugar

fold the flour mixture with the eggs and sugar a little at a time if
required add 1 3 tablespoons of water to obtain a smooth batter 

step 5 add the fruits carrots cinnamon orange strawberry essence

add the mixed fruits grated carrots cinnamon powder orange essence and
strawberry essence mix everything well

step 6 add vinegar and pour into the baking tin

add vinegar in the batter and transfer it immediately to the prepared baking
tin

step 7 bake for 35 minutes

now bake it for about 35 minutes at 200 degrees celsius till the cake is
done

step 8 servelet the cake cool down a bit then cut into pieces and servemake sure you
try this recipe rate it and let us know how it turned out to be

tips

1 to check whether the cake has been perfectly baked or not prick it with a fork or toothpick if it comes out clean the cake is done if not then bake for an additional 5 minute
2 you can also soak the mixed fruits and nuts in rum or grape juice overnight before adding to the cake for an enhanced flavour
