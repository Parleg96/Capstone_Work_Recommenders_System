step 2

remove pastry 2 sheets 25 x 25cm frozen puff pastry from your freezer and
thaw on bench for 3 5 minutes use a 6 7cm star shaped or fluted pastry cutter
to cut 16 shapes from pastry

step 3

arrange pastry shapes on prepared baking tray and brush each with the egg 1
egg lightly whisked bake in preheated oven for 4 5 minutes or until golden
remove from oven and cool

step 4

meanwhile combine sour lite cream 125mls 1/2 cup sour lite cream and
brown sugar 1 tbsp soft brown sugar in a bowl and stir until smooth then
gently stir through half the raspberries 300g 2 punnets raspberries
defrosted frozen raspberries may be used 

step 5

arrange 2 pastry shapes on each serving plate and top each with sour cream and
berry mixture top with remaining raspberries dust the remaining pastry
shapes with sifted icing sugar icing sugar to serve and place on top of
