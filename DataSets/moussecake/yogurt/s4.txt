a refreshing dessert recipe strawberry yoghurt mousse is a delicious sweet
dish that you can make this valentine s day for your partner cooked using
strawberries yoghurt and chocolate sponge this mousse recipe is easy to 
make try this easy dessert recipe and relish with your loved onescourtesy chef pankaj jha senior executive sous chef the suryaa new delhi

ingredients of strawberry yoghurt mousse

2 servings1 serving2 servings3 servings4 servings5 servings6 servings7

10 gm chocolate sponge
35 gm strawberry
5 gm sugar
1/2 cup water

50 gm whipped cream
5 gm gelatin
10 ml chilled water

for the main dish

10 ml strawberry puree

10 gm yoghurt curd 

how to make strawberry yoghurt mousse

step 1

soak the gelatine dark in cold water in a small mixing bowl and leave 5
minutes or until spongy set the bowl over a pan of hot water and stir until
the gelatine has dissolved remove from the heat and leave to cool

step 2

meanwhile put the strawberries and sugar in a bowl and mash with a fork

step 3

add whisked yoghurt and whipped cream with the above mixtures

step 4

add the dissolved gelatine and chill for at least 2 hours or until set

step 5

pour a little strawberry glaze over the top and decorate with fresh
strawberry serve chilled
